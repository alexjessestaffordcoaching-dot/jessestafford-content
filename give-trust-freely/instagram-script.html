Give Trust First. Withdraw It Specifically.

Platform: Instagram | Target: 3-5 sentences | Status: DRAFT

---

Most teams operate on a trust-as-currency model: people have to prove themselves first.

Here's what actually happens: They stop taking ownership. They ask permission instead of deciding. They bottle up feedback.

What if you flipped it?

Give trust as the starting position. Not blind trust—just assume people are capable and want to succeed. Then withdraw it only for specific behaviors that don't align.

You're managing the behavior, not the person.

That changes everything.
