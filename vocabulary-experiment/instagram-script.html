Instagram Script — The Vocabulary Experiment

Platform: Instagram | Target: 3-5 sentences, scroll-stopping hook Status: FINAL — Pending Jesse's approval

Script:

I used to say "I need to go argue with the helpdesk." Switched to "I'm going to have a conversation with them." Same task. My team responded differently just from the word.

Your vocabulary is setting the emotional temperature before anyone speaks. Pick one phrase you use that signals conflict or doubt. Notice it for a week. Then choose a different word. The shift happens faster than you think.
