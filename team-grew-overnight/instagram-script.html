Instagram Script — Team Growth / Delegation (approx. 65 seconds)

[DELIVERY NOTES: Direct to camera. Conversational, not formal. Measured pace. Let the pauses land.]

My team went from five people to twenty almost overnight.

And here is what I learned: the team did not fall behind because I promoted the wrong people, or because anyone stopped working hard.

They fell behind because of something I had never thought to hand off.

For years, I had been doing invisible work around my team. Tending relationships with internal stakeholders. Translating what we built into language the business understood. Making sure the right people knew why our work mattered.

I thought I was being helpful. I was. But I was also the only person who knew why any of that work existed.

When the team doubled and I got stretched, that work stopped happening. Not because anyone decided to stop. Because nobody knew it was supposed to start.

[Slight pause]

Here is the thing about delegation that nobody says: most leaders delegate the task. Almost nobody delegates the reason the task exists.

When the why lives only in your head, the work lives only in your hands.

So if your team or your role is growing — before you hand anything off, ask yourself: does anyone on my team know why this work matters?

Not just how to do it. Why it exists. Why the team depends on it.

If the answer is no, that is the conversation to have first.
