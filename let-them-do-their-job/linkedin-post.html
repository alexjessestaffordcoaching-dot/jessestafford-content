LinkedIn Post — Let Them Do Their Job

---

My engineer friend and I used the same car mechanic. He complained constantly about bad service. I always left happy.

Then I watched him at the counter. He had researched what was wrong and told the advisor exactly what to fix and how long it should take. The advisor — who knew more about that car than my friend ever would — got frustrated. My friend got mediocre service.

I walked up and said four words: "Will you figure it out?"

We lead the same way. We arrive at the answer, hand it over, then wonder why the experts on our teams seem to stop thinking. The truth is they did not stop thinking. We just stopped asking for it.

The most effective leaders I know describe problems, not solutions. What would change on your team if you stopped arriving with the answer?
