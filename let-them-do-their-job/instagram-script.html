Instagram Script — Let Them Do Their Job

---

[HOOK — first sentence must stop the scroll] The leaders who get the best work from experts almost never tell them what to do.

[STORY] My engineer friend complained constantly about his car mechanic. I watched him at the service counter one day: he had researched the problem, then told the advisor exactly what was wrong and how to fix it.

The advisor knew more about that car than either of us. He got frustrated. My friend got bad service.

[CONTRAST] I said four words: "Will you figure it out?" I got great service every time.

[INSIGHT] Same information. Different result. The only thing that changed was what I did with what I knew. I described a problem. He described a solution. Those are not the same thing.

[APPLICATION] Before your next conversation with an expert, try this: describe what is broken, not how to fix it. See what comes back.

  • Delivery Notes:
  • Start with the hook sentence as a single card or first line — it is the scroll-stopper
  • Pause briefly between the story and the contrast — let the contrast land
  • The final line ("Those are not the same thing") should be said slowly and definitively
  • Estimated length: 45-55 seconds
  • No emojis in the script text
  • If using slides: one sentence per card works well; contrast slide can be bold text only
